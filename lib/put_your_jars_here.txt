Drop the Prowide Integrator trial or production jars into this directory.
The build picks up every *.jar via fileTree, so version suffixes can differ.

Core (required by all examples):
  pw-swift-core-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-iso20022-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-swift-integrator-sdk-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar

Optional modules (include only those your trial covers):
  pw-swift-integrator-validation-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-swift-integrator-translations-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-swift-integrator-myformat-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-swift-integrator-cbpr-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-swift-integrator-score-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar
  pw-swift-integrator-sic-SRU2025-10.3.X.jar

After copying the jars, run:
  ./gradlew doctor
